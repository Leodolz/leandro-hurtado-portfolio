Elmer Leandro Hurtado Dolz : Personal Info

My hometown: Cochabamba

I currently reside in Cochabamba, Bolivia. Born and raised! I identify myself as Latino, and I am very happy with the heritage that involves, my country is not as popular as some other from the South America region. I would like to share that this country involves a giant set of different cultures (Almost 30 different cultures!), which means that there is a lot of diversity of mindset and a lot of cultural events. My hometown, Cochabamba, is located in the centered-region of Bolivia, which is mostly composed of valleys and have a generally warm temperature. This town is one of the three most important cities in Bolivia and therefore, it contains a high amount of population alongside with a bigger number of companies that offer different kinds of local products and services, it is more known for the places to eat as this town is very rich of people that innovated with national dishes such as: Silpancho, Pique Macho, Chicharron, etc.

Things to do in Cochabamba

  1. Visiting the Cochabamba's version of "Christ the Redeemer". You can go up by cable car
  2. Going to local restaurants that serve national dishes alongside with national drinks
  3. Strolling around the various amount of the city's parks
  4. Biking or having a walk around the "Lalay" lagoon

My hobbies

Below I talk about some of my favorite hobbies and passions I have in my personal life

  1. Video-games

    This would be my N#1 hobby as I grew up with video-games and its every day interaction with this hobby, determined my personality as time went by, having played with pretty much every console that went to my hands and having single-player video-games as my favorite category of video-games, I have gone through every genre it could offer me and I feel at ease in any kind of video-game I play.

    The time I dedicate to this hobby has changed throughout the different stages of my life, but I always play some at least on some nights of the week or some mornings on the weekends.

  2. Arts appreciation

    This may be a broad scope, but I really enjoy everything that involves art, either painting, music, movies, musicals, etc. Everything that involves expressing feelings, situations and mindsets through these different channels, I really enjoy that.

    This hobby has grown stronger over the last years as I have matured and can absorb better on what the artist wants to communicate, and I already play a musical instrument to feel even more connected with different artists, especially musical artists.

  3. Traveling

    Although this is not something you could do every day or even once a week, I find traveling a big passion whereas I want to dedicate as much time as I can while working, studying or focusing in other goals.

    Travelling has been a common practice in my family and has helped me create strong bonds with them, always having the curiosity and passion to see new places and learn from new mindsets. I am amazed with every travel I make, even if it is a local visit to a town nearby, I always find it enriching to my life and my mindset.

  4. Ping-pong

    I have started playing ping-pong since my dad purchased a table in my house, and played several times a day with my sister.

    This hobby grew when I was at college and made friends with some ping-pong players, I used to play every day after classes. Now I don't usually play, but from time to time I invite my friends to my home for some matches
